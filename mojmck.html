MójMCK

Your digital diary for university life.

Download APK

An unofficial companion app for students of Międzynarodowe Centrum Kształcenia (MCK) at Krakow University of Technology.

MójMCK gives you instant access to everything you need for your studies. Check your grades, monitor attendance, manage your schedule, and never miss a deadline with the built-in notes system.

Key Features

  • Grades & Attendance: View your subjects, track average grades (with weighted average calculation), and monitor your class attendance percentages.
  • Custom Schedule: Select your group and direction to set up a personalized timetable. Add, edit, and track time left until the end of the lesson.
  • Notes & Deadlines: Add tasks and notes to specific subjects. Set deadlines and receive push notifications so you never forget an assignment.
  • University News: Stay updated with the latest news and class topics directly in the app.
  • Secure & Fast: Secure authentication keeps your data safe. Everything is cached locally for fast, offline access. Dark mode supported.
